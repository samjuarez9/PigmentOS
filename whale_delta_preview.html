What Does Delta Mean?

Delta tells you how much the option moves for every $1 move in the stock.

Œî 0.35 = Option gains $0.35 for every $1 the stock goes up (speculative bet)
Œî 0.48 = Near the money, balanced risk/reward
Œî -0.72 = Deep in-the-money put, moves nearly with stock (hedging or conviction)

Low delta (0.1-0.3) = Lottery ticket, cheap but risky
Mid delta (0.3-0.5) = Sweet spot for speculation
High delta (0.5-1.0) = Expensive, moves like stock
